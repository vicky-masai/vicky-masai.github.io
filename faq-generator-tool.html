What is FAQ rich snippets?

FAQ rich snippets are a type of structured data markup that can be added to HTML code on a webpage. They provide search engines with specific information about frequently asked questions (FAQs) and their corresponding answers. This markup helps search engines understand the content better and may result in enhanced search results with expanded FAQ sections directly visible on the search engine results page (SERP).
